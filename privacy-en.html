When you add a contact, we store a record of that connection (identifiers of both users, date of last interaction, connection status). This data is subject to the impermanence policy described in Section 3.

2.4.1 Artificial Intelligence Processing

When you play ÉchoSpectre, phrase fragments extracted from your messages are transmitted to a third-party artificial intelligence service (a large language model) for linguistic evaluation (grammar, poetic quality and style scoring). This processing has the following characteristics:

  • Only isolated phrase fragments are transmitted — never entire conversations, metadata, user identifiers or any information that could identify you.
  • Processing occurs only during gameplay and the fragments are not permanently stored by the AI provider.
  • Messages are temporarily decrypted on the client side (on your device) for the time necessary to extract the fragments, which are then transmitted to the evaluation service via a secure connection (TLS).
  • This processing applies only to conversations for which both parties have given their explicit consent through the ÉchoSpectre privacy settings.
  • You may revoke your consent at any time from the ÉchoSpectre privacy settings. Revocation takes effect immediately for any future game session.

2.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your phone's contact list.
  • We do not use cookies, advertising trackers or behavioural analytics tools.
  • We do not collect geolocation data.
  • We do not display advertising and do not share any data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use any crash reporting system that transmits personal data.

5. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): A copy of all your personal data.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Deletion of your data. Messages already deleted under the impermanence policy cannot be erased a second time — they no longer exist.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18): Restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Export of your data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format. The export will include account data and any conversation fragments still available. Messages already deleted cannot be included.
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Objection to processing in certain circumstances.
  • Withdrawal of consent: For processing based on consent (profile photo, notifications), you may withdraw your consent at any time from the application settings.

To exercise any of these rights: rene@perihelie-labs.net

6. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption of communications using asymmetric and symmetric encryption algorithms compliant with current industry standards.
  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS).
  • Encryption of data at rest on Firebase servers.
  • Automated and irreversible deletion of data in accordance with the impermanence policy.
  • No backup copies of deleted data.
  • For ÉchoSpectre: temporary client-side decryption only, transmission of isolated fragments via TLS, no permanent storage by the AI provider.

7. Minors

Écho is intended for persons aged 16 and over. If we become aware that a user is under 16 years of age, we will delete their account and associated data without undue delay.
