Article 1 — Description of the Service

Écho is a personal messaging application founded on the principle of impermanence. The service enables the exchange of text messages, photographs and audio messages between users, operating in a manner that distinguishes it from conventional messaging applications:

  • Delivery delay: Messages are not delivered instantaneously. A delay of approximately 40 to 45 minutes applies between sending and receipt. This delay is an intentional and fundamental feature of the service.
  • Turn-based exchange: Exchanges operate on an alternating correspondence system. You may only send a new message after receiving a reply from your correspondent.
  • Automatic deletion: Messages, photographs and audio messages are automatically and irreversibly deleted after a defined period (see Article 5).
  • Disappearance of contacts: Contacts with whom no interaction has taken place for a prolonged period will automatically disappear from your list.

Écho is not an instant messaging service. Écho is not designed for urgent, professional or commercial communications, nor for any communication requiring guaranteed delivery or retention.

1.2 ÉchoSpectre

Écho includes an optional feature called ÉchoSpectre, a poetic word game. ÉchoSpectre allows users to play with phrases from their conversations by proposing word substitutions, evaluated by a third-party artificial intelligence service.

  • Mutual consent: To play on a conversation, both participants must have given their explicit consent through the ÉchoSpectre privacy settings. If either party has not consented, the conversation cannot be used in the game.
  • AI processing: Phrase fragments are transmitted to a third-party artificial intelligence service for linguistic evaluation. No personally identifiable data is transmitted (see the Privacy Policy for details).
  • Revocability: You may revoke your consent at any time. Revocation takes effect immediately for any future game session.
  • Impermanence compliance: ÉchoSpectre respects the impermanence policy. Messages that have exceeded their lifespan cannot be used in the game.

1.3 Music Box

Écho allows the exchange of short musical compositions between users. The Music Box is a feature that retains a persistent copy of these compositions in each participant's personal collection (sender and recipient).

  • Exception to impermanence: Musical compositions stored in the Music Box are not subject to the automatic deletion policy. They remain accessible until manually deleted by the user.
  • Data protection: The title and accompanying text of each composition are encrypted at rest. Musical notes, which do not constitute personal data, are stored unencrypted.
  • Independence of collections: Each user manages their own collection independently. Deleting a composition from your Music Box does not affect any copy held by the other participant.
  • Account deletion: If you delete your account, your entire Music Box is deleted.
